# From "Development of Personality in Early and Middle Adulthood: Set Like Plaster or Persistent Change?"

# The power-multipliers for "more" and "less" (1.125 and 0.888, respectively) correspond to roughly +/- 4% when the initial value is 50%.

>initial_traits
$loved=0.9 $clever=0.1 $fit=0.9 $open=0.8 $careful=0.5 $extrovert=0.6 $agreeable=0.6 $neurotic=0.5

>age_traits
~age_fit~age_open~age_careful~age_extrovert~age_agreeable~age_neurotic


>more_loved
$loved=&pow$loved{0.888}

>less_loved
$loved=&pow$loved{1.125}

>more_unloved
~less_loved

>more_hated
~less_loved
